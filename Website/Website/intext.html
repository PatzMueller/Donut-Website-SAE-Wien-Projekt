    OUR HISTORY

    CHOCOLATERIE DONUT

    from Columbia

    Our story begins in the lush heartlands of Colombia, where cacao trees sway in harmony with the wind. Inspired by the rich heritage of Colombian chocolate craftsmanship, our founder, Max Fischer, dreamed of sharing this decadence with the world. He envisioned a donut shop that not only celebrated the art of chocolate but also honored the land that nurtured it.

    With sustainability at its core, our shop proudly sources ethically grown cacao, ensuring every bite carries a taste of Colombia's bountiful landscapes and a promise of a brighter, greener future.

    HANDMADE DONUTS

    In our humble kitchen, tradition reigns supreme. Every donut is meticulously handcrafted, a testament to our founder's unwavering commitment to quality. From carefully selected flour to farm-fresh eggs and rich, velvety chocolate sourced directly from Colombia, each ingredient is chosen with utmost care. It's this dedication to using only the finest, singular ingredients that ensures every bite of our donuts is an indulgent experience, brimming with unmatched flavor and unparalleled quality.

    GreenGlaze

    CHOCOLATERIE DONUT

    Max Fischer

    Founder of GreenGlaze

    ¡Hola amigos! I'm Max Fischer, and let me share a bit of my story with you. For half my life, I've roamed the vibrant landscapes of Colombia, immersing myself in the rich tradition of chocolate-making. It was there, amidst the cacao groves and under the warm Colombian sun, that the idea for 'GreenGlaze' began to take shape in my heart.

    Sustainability isn't just a buzzword for me—it's a way of life. Inspired by the beauty of Colombia and deeply moved by its fragility, I knew that any venture of mine had to honor and protect this precious environment. That's why 'GreenGlaze' is more than just a donut shop—it's a commitment to sustainable practices, from sourcing ethically grown ingredients to minimizing waste at every step of the process.

    With each bite of our handcrafted delights, I hope to share not just the flavors of Colombia, but also the spirit of sustainability and compassion that drives us forward. So come, join us on this journey, and let's make the world a little greener, one donut at a time.
